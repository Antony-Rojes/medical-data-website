Check the injured person and identify where the bleeding is coming from. Stay calm and make sure the area is safe before giving first aid.

Place a clean cloth, bandage, or dressing directly over the bleeding wound. Use your hand to press firmly on the area to help slow the bleeding.

If blood soaks through, place another cloth or dressing on top and keep pressing. Do not remove the first pad. Maintain steady, firm pressure on the wound.

Wrap a bandage firmly around the injured area to hold the dressing in place. Make it snug but not too tight. Check fingers or toes for warmth and normal color to ensure blood flow is not blocked.
