Recognize choking. The person cannot speak, breathe, or cough normally. They may clutch their throat with their hands, which is the universal choking sign. Position yourself to the side and slightly behind the person immediately.

Stand to the side and slightly behind the person. Give five firm back blows between the shoulder blades using the heel of your hand. After each blow, check the mouth for the object.

Stand behind the person and wrap your arms around their waist. Place your fist just above the navel and below the ribcage. Grasp the fist with your other hand and give five sharp inward and upward thrusts. After each thrust, check the mouth for the object.
